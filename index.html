What We Offer

Our Hair Restoration Services

From minimally invasive procedures to advanced surgical techniques — we have the right solution for every stage of hair loss.

🔬
FUE Hair Transplant

Follicular Unit Extraction is the most sought-after technique today. Individual hair follicles are carefully extracted from the donor area and implanted into the thinning zones — leaving no linear scar and requiring minimal downtime.

→ Most Popular
✂️
FUT Hair Transplant

The FUT strip technique is ideal for patients who need a larger number of grafts in a single session. A thin strip of scalp is removed from the donor area, follicles are separated, and then transplanted to the bald region with precision.

→ High Graft Volume
💉
DHI Hair Transplant

Direct Hair Implantation uses a specialised Choi implanter pen to place each follicle directly into the scalp without pre-making incisions. The result is a denser, more natural look with faster recovery and minimal handling of grafts.

→ Scar-Free Method
🩸
PRP Therapy

Platelet Rich Plasma therapy uses growth factors from your own blood to stimulate dormant follicles and slow down hair loss. A non-surgical option that works well as a standalone treatment or as a complement to a transplant procedure.

→ Non-Surgical
🧔
Beard Transplant

Thin or patchy beard? Our beard transplant procedure fills in gaps and creates a fuller, naturally shaped beard that grows just like real facial hair — because it is real hair, taken from your own scalp donor area.

→ Facial Hair Restoration
👁️
Eyebrow Transplant

Sparse or uneven eyebrows caused by over-plucking, genetics, or medical conditions can be fully restored. We carefully transplant follicles to match your natural brow shape, angle, and growth direction for a seamless look.

→ Precision Restoration
